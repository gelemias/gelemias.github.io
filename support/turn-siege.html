Turn Siege — Support

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Thanks for playing Turn Siege! If you need help, found a bug, or have an idea for a new weapon, get in touch using the details below.

Contact

  • Email: gelemias@gmail.com

I usually reply within a few business days.

Reporting a problem

To help me resolve your issue quickly, please include:

  • The version of Turn Siege you are using.
  • Your device model (e.g. iPhone 16 Pro).
  • Your iOS version.
  • A short description of what happened and the steps to reproduce it — which round, which front, which weapons equipped.
  • Screenshots, if relevant.

Common questions

  • I lost my progress. Turn Siege saves your scrap, arsenal and streak locally on your device. Progress can be lost if the app's local storage is cleared (e.g. offloading the app without "Keep Documents & Data", or restoring from a backup that predates your save). There is no cloud sync or account recovery.
  • My daily streak reset. The scrap-multiplier streak requires playing on consecutive calendar days; missing a day resets it back to the base multiplier.
  • Feature requests. Always welcome — email me with "Turn Siege feature request" in the subject line.
