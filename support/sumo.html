Reporting a problem

To help me resolve your issue quickly, please include:

  • The version of Sumo you are using (shown in the app's settings).
  • Your device model (e.g. iPhone 16 Pro).
  • Your iOS version.
  • A short description of what happened and the steps to reproduce it.
  • Screenshots, if relevant.
