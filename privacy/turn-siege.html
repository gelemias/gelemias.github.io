Turn Siege — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This privacy policy applies to Turn Siege, an isometric defense game for iOS developed by Guillermo Rodriguez Delgado. Turn Siege is rated 13+.

Data collection

Turn Siege does not collect, store or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-in and no network connection required to play. All progress — scrap, unlocked weapons, upgrades and daily streaks — is saved locally on your device (and in your personal iCloud account if you enable device backups, which are managed entirely by Apple).

Third-party services

Turn Siege does not use third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, crash reporting or tracking tools of any kind. The game contains no ads and no in-app purchases.

Data sharing

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with third parties.

Age rating

Turn Siege is rated 13+ and is not directed at children. As stated above, the app does not collect data from any user regardless of age.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this policy will be updated here before the change takes effect, and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact me at gelemias@gmail.com or visit the Turn Siege support page.
