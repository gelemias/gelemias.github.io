Last updated: July 17, 2026

This page describes the general privacy practices for apps published by Guillermo Rodriguez Delgado ("I", "me"). Each app also has its own dedicated privacy policy — see the app directory below for specifics.

General principles

  • My apps are designed to collect as little data as possible.
  • I do not sell, rent or trade personal data with third parties.
  • Data that an app needs to function is processed on your device whenever possible.
  • My apps do not display third-party advertising.

Contact

If you have any questions about privacy in any of my apps, contact me at gelemias@gmail.com or visit the support page.
