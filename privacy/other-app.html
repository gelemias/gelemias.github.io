Other App — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Other App app for iOS, developed by Guillermo Rodriguez Delgado.

Data collection

Other App does not collect, store or transmit any personal data. All app data stays on your device (and in your personal iCloud account if you enable device backups or syncing, which are managed entirely by Apple).

Third-party services

Other App does not use third-party analytics, advertising frameworks or tracking tools of any kind.

Data sharing

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with third parties.

Children's privacy

Other App does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this policy will be updated here before the change takes effect, and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact me at gelemias@gmail.com or visit the support page.
